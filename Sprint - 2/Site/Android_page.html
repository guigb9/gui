Mobile app













The TechCare application aims to bring the contact between doctor and patient closer, specifically for cases of COVID-19, this way, patients can answer questions with a team, or pass a question to a doctor during a home quarantine period.

































Our application will also offer the user the possibility to access notes written by health professionals on any subject and send your questions to be answered in the future.
